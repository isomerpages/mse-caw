<p>We thank all our valued partners for your strong support!</p>
<p>While most events under Go Green SG are open to the public, MSE, along
with our partners are also celebrating Go Green SG with a wide array of
internal events to encourage sustainability within our organisations!</p>
<p>For example, MSE and its statutory boards, NEA, PUB and SFA will be co-hosting
the inaugural MSE Family Go Green Week. It comprises sustainability-related
activities to encourage our staff to reduce waste, water and energy use,
and to purchase local produce.</p>
<p>Here are some other ways our partners are Going Green, their way!</p>
