**Embark on a fun quest around the airport to discover fun facts you’d never know! After the quest, play airport-themed games at Changi Experience Studio for a fun-filled time!**

Date: 1- 31 July, 11:00 am - 6:00 pm<br>
Admission: $50 per person<br>
Venue: Changi Experience Studio<br>
Organiser: Changi Airport Group/Changi Experience Studio

Embark on a fun Amazing Airport Quest and bond as a team while discovering about the airport’s fun facts and sustainability efforts which you never knew! In your own teams, go on a self-discovery trail across Jewel and Terminal 3 to uncover clues and conquer challenges. For instance, did you know that we have our own Changi Scent and Changi Bougainvillea? Did you know how the trees in the old Terminal 1 carpark were upcycled? Along the quest, discover unique local shops and capture memorable moments with your teammates!  
 
 After the quest, continue to have a fun-filled time at Changi Experience Studio, a digital attraction themed around Changi Airport. There, uncover the behind-the-scenes secrets on what makes Changi Airport tick like clockwork through interactive exhibits, exciting games and shows.
