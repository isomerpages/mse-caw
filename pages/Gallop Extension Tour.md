**In this guided walk through the Gallop Extension of the Singapore Botanical Gardens, you will get to view unique collections and galleries along the trail.**

**Date:** 15 July, 9:00 am<br>
**Admission:** Free <br>
**Venue:** Singapore Botanic Gardens<br>
**Organiser:** National Parks Board

This tour begins at the Visitor Services Centre at Gallop Extension and will take visitors through the OCBC Arboretum to view the dipterocarp collection. Visitors will also experience the Mingxin Foundation Ramblerâ€™s Ridge, Forest Discovery Centre @ OCBC Arboretum and the Botanical Art Gallery.

This tour is recommended for 6 years old and above. No pets are allowed.

Registration is required by 8.45am at the Gallop Visitor Services Desk (Nearest Entrance: Gallop Gate). Limited slots on a first-come-first-served basis.
