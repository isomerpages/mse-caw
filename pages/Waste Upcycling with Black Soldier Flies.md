**Date:** 22 and 23 June, 12:30 pm – 2:00 pm  <br> 
**Admission:** $8/pax <br> 
**Venue:** Nippon Koi Farm, 51 Jalan Lekar  <br> 
**Organiser:** Kranji Countryside Association   

Learn about the black soldier fly and its role in waste management and nutrient recycling.  

This workshop includes hands-on activities and demonstrations, allowing participants to gain practical skills in the rearing black soldier flies. This knowledge can be applied in various contexts, such as home composting.  

This activity is suitable for individuals with mobility issues.
