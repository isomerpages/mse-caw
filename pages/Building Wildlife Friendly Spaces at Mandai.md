**Admission:** Free (registration required)<br>
**Venue:** Mandai Wildlife West<br>
**Organiser:** Mandai Wildlife Group

Mandai Wildlife Reserve is home to a myriad of native plants and animals. Join us on an insightful tour to discover how we balanced development in Mandai precinct with wildlife protection.<br> 
Our tour kicks off with an indoor session where you'll get a peek into our unconventional methods for protecting biodiversity during the construction of the Mandai precinct. These include measures such as construction of a wildlife bridge and rope bridges to enhance ecological connectivity.<br>
 After the indoor sharing, we'll take you on a guided tour of the stunning green spaces in Mandai Wildlife West and its sustainable features.
Don't miss out on this never-seen-before experience!
