**Date:** 14, 21, 28 June, 2:00 pm – 4:00 pm<br>
**Admission:** Free (registration required)<br>
**Venue:** Earth Observatory of Singapore, Nanyang Technological University, 50 Nanyang Avenue, Block N2-01a-15<br>
**Organiser:** Earth Observatory of Singapore, Nanyang Technological University Singapore 

The Earth Observatory of Singapore (EOS) invites participants to learn about EOS’ activities, and to see how EOS is tackling sustainability issues of the day.

The visit will include a walkthrough of EOS laboratories to learn how its scientists are developing solutions to adapt to future climate change.

Participants will also get to think about sustainability in a broader perspective by considering other key aspects such as resilience to natural hazards.&nbsp;

Registration for this tour is on a first come, first served basis. The tour is suitable for secondary school students and adults (including individuals with mobility issues).
