**Experience Earth Observatory of Singapore's research and projects and how they are tackling sustainability issues of the day**


**Date:** 4, 5 July, 3:00 - 5:00pm<br>
**Admission:** Free (registration required)<br>
**Venue:** Earth Observatory of Singapore. 50 Nanyang Avenue<br>
**Organiser:** Earth Observatory of Singapore

The Earth Observatory of Singapore (EOS) Tour is an opportunity for participants to catch a glimpse of EOS' activities, and seeing first-hand how EOS is tackling the sustainability issues of the day.
Participants will discover the research from EOS that promotes a safer and more sustainable future. The visit will include a walk through the EOS laboratories to learn about how EOS scientists are looking at past climates and at the solutions to adapt to future climate change. We will also think about sustainability is a broader sense by considering other key aspects such as our resilience to natural hazards.
The tour is open for all, but the content would be more suited for students from secondary schools and adults. <br>
Due to limited capacity and participants will be accepted upon registration on a first come, first served basis. Another slot may be opened on 5 July if the 4 July tour is oversubscribed.
